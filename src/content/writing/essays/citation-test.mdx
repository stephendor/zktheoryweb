### Accessibility checks

- **Keyboard**: Tab to each citation trigger, press Enter/Space to toggle, Escape to dismiss.
- **Screen reader**: Trigger announces `"View citation: {title}"`; tooltip has `role="tooltip"`.
- **Reduced motion**: Set `prefers-reduced-motion: reduce` in OS settings — tooltip should appear instantly with no transition.
- **Right-edge flip**: In a narrow viewport, the tooltip should flip right-aligned rather than overflow.
